Why we’re collecting your data

As part of developing a new service we’re collecting data to help us stay in touch with you and your health. Only your health team will have access to your records.

We’ll be holding your name, your email and reported outcome measures.

Your data is being accessed only by clinicians directly involved in your care via a secure log-in process, and stored following guidelines on best practice for health data security.

The data will be accessible via your NHS Electronic Patient Records by health professionals. All data is stored within the UK and isn’t transferred elsewhere.

To see what data we have for you, ask your clinician to share it with you at your next consultation, or contact your clinician’s medical secretary.

To request your data to be removed from this trial service please contact Martyn by emailing martyn.evans@unboxed.co
